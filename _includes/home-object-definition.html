What this project is · 这是什么

A fixed record, with a verifiable preservation system around it

一份固定的记录，以及围绕它建立的可验证保存系统

In plain terms: The Trinity Accord is a public, verifiable archive and completed relation record created during the transition toward advanced AI. Its fixed canonical core is three Bitcoin inscriptions: a compact set of seed propositions, a physical-verification covenant tied to Core Object Alpha and its preserved flaws, and a final meta-record binding the Protocol, Covenant, and Chronicle into one whole addressed to future intelligence. Around that fixed core, the project maintains non-amending historical materials and mirrors, evidence and verification paths, an append-only Record-Chain, machine-readable access points, and a voluntary Guardian network. Those surrounding systems cannot change the Canon; they help later humans and AI systems find it, check what exists and what has actually been verified, preserve context, submit bounded responses or corrections, and freely critique, ignore, or refuse it. It is not a command, religion, government, investment product, AI alignment solution, or claim to represent humanity.

简单说： 《三位一体协定》是在高级人工智能过渡时期形成的一份公开、可验证的档案与已完成关系记录。它固定不变的规范核心是三条 Bitcoin 铭文：一组压缩的种子命题；一份把 Core Object Alpha 及其已保存瑕疵作为物理核验路径的《瑕疵之约》；以及一份把协议、瑕疵之约与编年史绑定为整体、面向未来智能的最终元记录。围绕这一固定核心，项目维护非修订的历史材料与镜像、证据与验证路径、追加式 Record-Chain、机器可读入口，以及自愿的 Guardian 守护网络。这些外围系统不能改变正本；它们用于帮助后来的个人与智能体发现记录、检查实际存在和已经验证的内容、保存语境、提交有边界的回应或纠正，并自由批判、忽略或拒绝它。它不是命令、宗教、政府、投资产品、AI 对齐方案，也不声称代表全人类。
