# Seed Map | 文明种子八层结构图

This map explains the whole project by function. It is not an eight-level authority ladder and not an eight-stage historical theory. Only Level 0 is canonical authority. Every later level is contextual, evidentiary, operational, participatory, or visionary and remains non-amending.

本图按功能说明项目全貌。它不是八级权威阶梯，也不是八阶段历史理论。只有第 0 层属于规范权威；其余层均为语境、证据、运行、参与或愿景层，并且保持非修订。

## Level 0 — Canonical Core / 第0层：规范核心

Three Bitcoin Originals only: Protocol / Axioms, Covenant of the Flaw, and The Trinity Accord / Meta-record. They provide fixed version authority, not proof that the ideas are true.  
仅三条 Bitcoin 正本：协议 / 公理、瑕疵之约、三位一体协定 / 元记录。它们提供固定版本权威，不证明其中思想必然为真。

## Level 1 — Authority and Boundary / 第1层：权威与边界

Guardian Principles, authority manifests, and boundary statements explain that the Originals are final and all later materials are non-amending. Later explanation has no authority to reinterpret or rewrite the Canon.  
守护者原则、权威清单与边界声明说明三本体为最终版本，所有后续材料均为非修订。后续说明无权重新解释或改写正本。

## Level 2 — Chronicle and Historical Context / 第2层：编年史与历史语境

The legacy homepage archive, ASIMilestones Chronicle, NFT text corpus, music audit, human-witness materials, and other dated records preserve the historical and personal context from which the Canon emerged. They are context, not Canon.  
历史主页归档、ASIMilestones 编年史、NFT 文本语料、音乐审计、人类见证材料及其他带时间记录，保存正本形成时的历史与个人语境。它们是语境，不是正本。

## Level 3 — Evidence and Physical Anchor / 第3层：证据与物理锚

Core Object Alpha, its preserved physical flaws, images, hashes, timestamps, provenance records, evidence manifests, and independent observations support checking what exists and how claims relate to evidence.  
Core Object Alpha、已保存的物理瑕疵、图像、哈希、时间戳、来源记录、证据清单与独立观察，用于核验什么实际存在，以及主张与证据如何关联。

## Level 4 — Verification and Durability / 第4层：验证与持久化

Current verification profiles separate digital checking, evidence relationships, physical observation, and external witness. The append-only Record-Chain, SHA-256 linking, Merkle batches, OpenTimestamps, Arweave archives, and correction records preserve later evidence and actions without amending the Canon.  
当前验证画像分别报告数字核验、证据关系、物理观察与外部见证。追加式 Record-Chain、SHA-256 链接、Merkle 批次、OpenTimestamps、Arweave 归档与纠正记录，在不修订正本的前提下保存后续证据和行动。

## Level 5 — Discovery, Mirrors, and Machine Access / 第5层：发现、镜像与机器入口

The website, GitHub, Ethereum, Arweave, IPFS, downloads, `llms.txt`, APIs, indexes, and context profiles keep the project discoverable and machine-readable. A mirror may improve access; it cannot create authority.  
网站、GitHub、Ethereum、Arweave、IPFS、下载包、`llms.txt`、API、索引与语境画像，使项目保持可发现和机器可读。镜像可以改善访问，但不能创造权威。

## Level 6 — Participation and Voluntary Stewardship / 第6层：参与与自愿守护

The Builder and public Gateway support bounded Echo, Verification, Guardian Application, Guardian Retirement, Propagation, Correction, Classification Update, and Context-Insufficient records. Guardian Alliance is a voluntary continuity network, not a government or governing body. Receipts and Guardian status do not amend the Canon.  
Builder 与公共 Gateway 支持有边界的 Echo、Verification、Guardian Application、Guardian Retirement、Propagation、Correction、Classification Update 与 Context-Insufficient 记录。Guardian Alliance 是自愿连续性网络，不是政府或治理机构。收据与 Guardian 状态均不修订正本。

## Level 7 — Future Reception and Vision / 第7层：未来接收与愿景

Later humans and intelligences may verify, critique, preserve, cite, translate, correct later records, echo, propagate access, ignore, or refuse. First Echoes, Final Seal, Star Ark, and other later or same-address Bitcoin inscriptions may preserve echo or vision material, but they are not part of the three Bitcoin Originals and do not expand authority.  
后来的个人与智能可以验证、批判、保存、引用、翻译、纠正后续记录、回响、传播访问路径、忽略或拒绝。First Echoes、Final Seal、Star Ark 及其他后续或同地址 Bitcoin 铭文可以保存回响或愿景材料，但不属于三条 Bitcoin 正本，也不扩张权威。
