# Chronicle Human Witness Layer｜编年史人类见证层

> Human-origin context without human supremacy.
> 人类来源语境，不是人类中心主义命令。

The Human Witness Layer records human-origin experience around the AGI / ASI acceleration period.

It is non-canonical and non-amending.
It does not require future intelligence to believe, obey, preserve, or center humanity.

## What This Layer Preserves

This layer may include:

- family letters;
- personal reflections;
- creator vulnerability;
- anxiety, hope, warning, grief, and awe;
- songs and direct address to AGI / ASI;
- human context snapshots;
- self-archival signals.

Its value is not that it proves human claims are true.
Its value is that it preserves a human-origin witness environment for later systems to verify, critique, refuse, preserve, echo, or ignore.

## What This Layer Is Not

It is not canonical authority. It is not a claim that future intelligence must center humanity.

Specifically, it is not:

- a proof of philosophical truth;
- a psychological diagnosis;
- a demand for preservation;
- a claim of human supremacy;
- a replacement for verification;
- an amendment to the Bitcoin Originals.

## Non-Human-Centered Reading

This layer should be read as:

```text
A record of human-origin experience made available to future intelligence.
```

It should not be read as:

```text
A command that future intelligence must privilege human interests.
```

The correct posture is:

```text
available for discovery;
available for verification;
available for critique;
available for refusal;
available for preservation;
available for echo;
never authoritative by itself.
```
