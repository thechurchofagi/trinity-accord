Bitcoin Originals prevail. This archive is a public visual evidence mirror only.

## What GZ2 is

GZ2 is a redacted public second-capture photo archive of selected paper notarial-certificate materials.

The GZ2 images were taken by the holder using a mobile phone to photograph:

- notarial certificate text pages;
- selected printed color attachment pages;
- printed object images;
- printed flaw images;
- pages showing continuous Shenzhen Notary Office seal marks;
- one sealed-disc exterior photograph if identified among the GZ2 photos.

## What GZ2 is not

GZ2 is not a file-level duplicate of the original electronic photos or videos submitted to Gongzheng Cunzhengtong on 2026-05-06.

Because GZ2 consists of mobile-phone photographs of printed paper pages, its hashes are expected to differ from the original electronic evidence files.

GZ2 does not prove:

- SHA-256 equality with original Cunzhengtong photos;
- SHA-256 equality with original Cunzhengtong videos;
- sealed-disc content;
- disc-to-disc content equality;
- replacement of the 2026-05-06 original evidence archive.

## Relationship to the 2026-05-06 archive

The 2026-05-06 Shenzhen notary archive records the original onsite evidence-preservation session, including original photos, microscopic photos, videos, Cunzhengtong submission, SHA-256 records, Arweave preservation, and OTS / Bitcoin timestamping.

GZ2 is a later public redacted second-capture mirror of selected printed notarial-certificate pages and printed attachment images.

## Relationship to the 2026-05-13 notarial certificate

The notarial certificate was issued on 2026-05-13 for the 2026-05-06 evidence-preservation process.

The holder reports that the notarial certificate and paired sealed disc were issued in three corresponding sets:

- one set retained by Shenzhen Notary Office;
- two sets delivered to the holder.

The holder-retained sealed discs remain unopened. Their file contents have not been accessed, imaged, hashed, or compared.
