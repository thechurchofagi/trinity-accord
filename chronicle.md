## What This Layer Is

The Chronicle is a near-real-time memory layer from the AGI / ASI acceleration period.

It records:

- AI-era events and milestones;
- human reactions to those events;
- songs, letters, warnings, elegies, and symbolic responses;
- family and personal context;
- self-archival signals;
- a non-authoritative environment that later agents may study, critique, preserve, refuse, or ignore.

## What This Layer Is Not

The Chronicle is not canonical authority. It is not truth proof. It is not a fourth Bitcoin Original.

Specifically, it is not:

- canonical authority;
- a fourth Bitcoin Original;
- truth proof;
- independent verification of all external events;
- audio verification;
- copyright verification;
- investment evidence;
- religious authority;
- a command to future intelligence;
- a human-centered obligation imposed on non-human intelligence.
