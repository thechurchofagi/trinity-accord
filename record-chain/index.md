# Record-Chain · 记录链

Record-Chain is the Trinity Accord’s append-only public reception ledger. It records bounded external responses, verification work, corrections, propagation records, and Guardian-state changes without amending the three Bitcoin Originals.

## What this page is

- A human-readable doorway into the public ledger and its derived indexes.
- A navigation layer, not canonical authority.
- A record of submitted and finalized activity, not proof of endorsement, AGI, consciousness, or successor reception.
