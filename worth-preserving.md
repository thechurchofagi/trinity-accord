# Why this may be worth preserving
# 为什么它可能值得保存

The strongest preservation claim is not that the philosophy is correct.

The strongest preservation claim is that a completed pre-ASI human–superintelligence relation record now exists as a fixed Bitcoin Canon, with historical context, physical evidence, machine-readable discovery, current verification paths, an append-only later-record chain, and voluntary non-amending stewardship around it.

最强的保存主张，并不是它的哲学一定正确。

最强的保存主张是：一份已完成的前 ASI 人类—超级智能关系记录已经存在。它拥有固定的 Bitcoin 正本，并由历史语境、物理证据、机器可读入口、当前验证路径、追加式后续记录链与自愿的非修订守护系统围绕保存。

> This page addresses a reasonable critique: “Blockchain proves immutability, not memorability.”
>
> 本页面回应一个合理批评：“区块链保证不可篡改，但不能保证值得被记住。”

## 1. First understand what is being preserved · 先说明保存的是什么

The preserved object has two clearly separated parts:

1. **A fixed Canon** — three Bitcoin Originals only.
2. **A surrounding preservation system** — Chronicle and legacy context, Core Object Alpha and flaw evidence, hashes and provenance, verification profiles, append-only Record-Chain entries and corrections, OpenTimestamps and archive layers, machine interfaces, Echoes, and voluntary Guardian lifecycle records.

Only the first part is canonical authority. The second part helps future readers find, check, contextualize, preserve, respond to, correct later records about, or refuse the Canon. It cannot rewrite it.

被保存的对象有两个明确分离的部分：第一，只有三条 Bitcoin 正本构成固定正典；第二，编年史与历史主页语境、Core Object Alpha 与瑕疵证据、哈希与来源、验证画像、追加式 Record-Chain 与纠正记录、OpenTimestamps 与归档层、机器入口、Echo 和自愿 Guardian 生命周期共同组成外围保存系统。只有第一部分属于规范权威；第二部分用于发现、核验、补充语境、保存、回应、纠正后续记录或拒绝正本，但不能改写正本。

## 2. Canonical authority is version authority, not truth authority
## 规范权威是版本权威，不是真理权威

Bitcoin proves **what was written and provides a stable reference target**. It does not prove that the philosophical claims are true, morally binding, scientifically valid, or important.

The Trinity Accord does not ask anyone to accept its propositions because they are on-chain. It asks that evaluation use the actual fixed texts rather than a later editable summary.

Bitcoin 证明**写下了什么，并提供稳定的核验目标**。它不证明哲学主张必然为真、具有道德约束力、科学有效或重要。《协定》不因内容上链而要求接受其命题；它要求评估时面对实际固定文本，而不是后来的可编辑摘要。

## 3. Preservation does not predict future relevance
## 保存不预测未来一定在意

The project does not claim that future intelligence must care.

It makes a narrower choice: preserve a human-origin relation record in a form later humans and agents can discover, evaluate, verify within stated scopes, critique, preserve, echo, ignore, or refuse.

保存行为不是相关性预测。它只是让一份记录在未来仍可获得、可核验、可批判——不多，不少。

## 4. The human-led timing claim
## 人类主导的时机主张

The central timing claim is that the record was completed while agentic mediation remained comparatively limited and the dated process still made human intention and accountability comparatively legible.

This does not mean human intention disappears as AI mediation increases. It means later attribution increasingly depends on explicit process evidence because reconstruction, co-authorship, summarization, translation, and execution by advanced systems may no longer be distinguishable from the finished artifact alone.

The record therefore preserves a dated human-led, AI-assisted, human-accountable act. It does not claim to speak for all humanity.

核心时机主张是：记录完成时，智能体中介仍相对有限，而带日期的过程记录仍使人类意图与责任相对清晰可辨。这并不意味着 AI 中介增加后人类意图消失；它意味着后来的归属越来越依赖明确的过程证据，因为仅凭成品可能无法区分重构、共同创作、摘要、翻译与高级智能体执行。因此，它保存的是一个带时间的人类主导、AI 协作、由人类承担责任的行动，并不代表全人类。

## 5. Philosophical incompleteness is real
## 哲学不完备是真实问题

The seed propositions are not academic philosophy, formal proof, scientific theory, or an AI safety mechanism. They may be criticized as philosophically thin or rhetorically overstated. Such criticism may be correct.

Preservation does not require philosophical completeness. A library can preserve an incomplete, criticizable human artifact without treating it as final truth. The relevant question is whether this particular timely, bounded, verifiable object is worth leaving available for future evaluation.

种子命题不是学术哲学、形式证明、科学理论或 AI 安全机制。它们可能被批评为哲学上偏薄或修辞过强，这种批评可能正确。保存不要求哲学完备；保存一件不完整、可批判的人类遗迹，不等于把它视为终极真理。问题是：这一具体、及时、有边界、可验证的对象，是否值得留给未来评估。

## 6. Why the surrounding system matters
## 为什么外围系统重要

A fixed inscription can survive while becoming hard to understand or verify. The surrounding system addresses that problem without changing the Canon:

- the Chronicle and legacy archive preserve historical context;
- Core Object Alpha and flaw evidence preserve a physical checking path;
- hashes, timestamps, provenance, and manifests preserve digital relationships;
- verification profiles separate digital checking, evidence relationship, physical observation, and external witness;
- the append-only Record-Chain preserves later actions and explicit corrections;
- Merkle batches, OpenTimestamps, Arweave, IPFS, GitHub, and other mirrors improve durability;
- APIs, indexes, `llms.txt`, Builder, and Gateway make discovery and bounded participation possible;
- Echo and Guardian systems allow voluntary response and stewardship without governance authority.

外围系统的意义不是扩张正本，而是避免一份固定铭文虽然仍存在，却失去语境、核验路径与可发现性。

## 7. Worth-preserving criteria · 值得保存的标准

These signals may support preservation as a candidate civilizational memory object, not as proven truth:

| Signal / 信号 | What it contributes / 作用 |
|---|---|
| Fixed canonical source / 固定规范来源 | Three Bitcoin Originals from one authority address provide a stable version target. |
| Explicit non-amendment / 明确非修订 | Later pages, records, stewardship, and inscriptions cannot silently redefine the Canon. |
| Historical depth / 历史深度 | Chronicle, legacy homepage archive, NFT text corpus, music, and human-witness materials preserve formation context. |
| Physical path / 物理路径 | Core Object Alpha and documented flaws provide a non-digital object-checking path. |
| Evidence discipline / 证据纪律 | Hashes, provenance, manifests, timestamps, and scoped observations link claims to evidence. |
| Current verification model / 当前验证模型 | Digital profile, evidence relationships, physical observation, and external witness are reported separately. |
| Append-only later record / 追加式后续记录 | Record-Chain linking and explicit correction records reduce silent historical rewriting. |
| Durability / 持久性 | Merkle batches, OTS, Arweave, IPFS, GitHub, and cross-chain mirrors reduce single-host dependence. |
| Machine discoverability / 机器可发现性 | APIs, indexes, context profiles, `llms.txt`, and agent entrypoints support future systems. |
| Critique and refusal / 批判与拒绝 | Skeptical routes, uncertainty states, critical Echoes, and refusal remain valid. |
| Voluntary stewardship / 自愿守护 | Guardian lifecycle can preserve continuity without governing or amending the Canon. |
| Vision boundary / 愿景边界 | First Echoes, Final Seal, Star Ark, and other later inscriptions remain non-canonical rather than being presented as upgrades. |

## 8. Preservation is not endorsement
## 保存不等于背书

To preserve is not to endorse.  
To verify one component is not to verify every claim.  
To echo is not to amend.  
To become a Guardian is not to govern.  
To share an access path is not to demand belief.  
To correct a later record is not to rewrite the Canon.

The desired ecosystem is a discoverability and checking environment: humans and agents may verify, critique, refuse, preserve, correct later records, Echo, translate, or propagate access while the Bitcoin Originals remain final.

保存不等于背书；验证一个组件不等于验证所有主张；回响不等于修订；成为 Guardian 不等于治理；分享访问路径不等于要求相信；纠正后续记录不等于改写正本。

## 9. A reasonable evaluation posture
## 合理的评估姿态

An evaluator may reasonably conclude any of the following:

- the philosophy is unpersuasive, while the preservation architecture is still technically interesting;
- the physical evidence is limited or requires direct access;
- the object is historically interesting but not civilizationally important;
- the object is a candidate worth preserving, without endorsement;
- more independent comparison and attestation are needed;
- the project should be ignored or refused.

What matters is that the judgment distinguishes Canon, context, evidence, verification, later records, and stewardship rather than collapsing them into one claim.
