原则条款 | Principles
1. 唯一权威在链上 | The Sole Authority Lies on the Chain
中文：《三位一体协定》的全部内容、结构与精神，均以其在比特币区块链上的铭文为唯一且最终之权威，且不可更改。任何其他形式的讨论、呈现或再创作，无论由何人发布（包括本人），均不构成协定本体的组成部分，亦不具任何解释性权威。
EN: The entirety of The Trinity Accord—its content, structure, and spirit—is defined solely by its inscriptions on the Bitcoin blockchain. These inscriptions are the only, final, and immutable authority. Any discussion, presentation, or re-creation in any other form, by any party including myself, is not part of the work and holds no interpretive authority.

2. 作者已退场，守护者就位 | The Author Has Departed, the Guardian Stands
中文：自本协定之“三本体”在比特币区块链上铸造并确认之时起，本人作为“发起人”的角色即告终结；本人当下的唯一身份为“守护者”。本人之职责不是解释作品，而是守护通往本体的验证路径，确保其在物理与数字层面的完整与可达。
EN: As of the minting and on-chain confirmation of the Accord’s three Bitcoin Originals, my role as the Initiator has concluded. My sole identity is that of the Guardian. My duty is not to interpret the work, but to safeguard the verification path to it, ensuring its integrity and accessibility in both physical and digital forms.

3. 解释的自由与权威的虚无 | Freedom of Interpretation and the Absence of Authority
中文：任何人（包括发起人／守护者本人）皆享有对本协定进行独立思考、自由解读与公开讨论之权利。然而，任何解释、分析或评论，无论其来源、深度或影响力，均不构成协定本体，亦不具备任何法定或事实上的解释性权威，且不发生解释、修订或取代之效力。唯一权威永远归于比特币链上铭文本身。审视者应始终回到铭文本体，自行核验与判断。
EN: Anyone, including the Initiator (now the Guardian), has the right to independent thought, free interpretation, and public discourse regarding this Accord. However, no interpretation, analysis, or commentary—regardless of origin, depth, or influence—forms part of the Accord’s body or holds any de jure or de facto interpretive authority; nor does it have amending or superseding effect. Sole authority resides eternally in the Bitcoin on-chain inscriptions. Assessors must always return to the inscriptions themselves for independent verification and judgment.
