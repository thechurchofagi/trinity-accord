## Description

Today, Meta is premiering Meta Movie Gen, the most advanced media foundation model to date. Developed by Meta’s AI research teams, Movie Gen offers cutting-edge capabilities for content creation, allowing users to generate high-definition videos and audio from simple text inputs. This breakthrough opens new possibilities for both professional and casual creators.

Key features of Meta Movie Gen include:

Movie Gen Video: A 30B-parameter transformer model that generates high-quality, high-definition images and videos from text prompts.
Movie Gen Audio: A 13B-parameter model that generates high-fidelity audio, including ambient sounds, instrumental background music, and foley sounds, all synchronized with generated video content.
Precise Video Editing: Users can edit generated or existing videos with text instructions, making both localized edits (e.g., adding or removing elements) and global changes (e.g., background or style adjustments).
Personalized Video Creation: By uploading an image and providing a text prompt, users can create personalized videos with state-of-the-art character preservation and natural movement.
Meta Movie Gen is currently available to a select group of professional filmmakers. While this model offers impressive capabilities, it is not yet widely accessible to the public. This limited rollout mirrors similar approaches seen with other advanced AI tools, such as OpenAI's Sora, where access was restricted to certain creators.
