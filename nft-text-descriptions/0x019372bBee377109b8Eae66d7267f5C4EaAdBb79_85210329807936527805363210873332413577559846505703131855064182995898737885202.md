August 13, 2024: A new large language model, Grok-2, is released, demonstrating state-of-the-art reasoning capabilities and surpassing both Claude 3.5 Sonnet and GPT-4-Turbo on the LMSYS leaderboard. Kwon acknowledges the potential "catastrophic risks" associated with AGI, including extreme persuasive power, cyberattacks, support for dangerous weapons, and AI autonomy. He emphasizes OpenAI's commitment to safety research and responsible deployment of AGI technology.
