NFT Description:

Event Overview:

On December 25, 2024,today, the Qwen Team unveiled QVQ, a groundbreaking open-weight model for multimodal reasoning built upon Qwen2-VL-72B. This release marks a significant advancement in AI's capacity for visual understanding and complex problem-solving, particularly in scientific domains. QVQ's ability to perform careful step-by-step reasoning and achieve a score of 70.3 on the MMMU benchmark, alongside substantial improvements in math-related benchmarks, signals a new era where AI can tackle university-level, multidisciplinary challenges.

Technological Breakthrough:

State-of-the-Art Performance: QVQ achieves a 70.3 score on the MMMU benchmark, significantly outperforming its predecessor, Qwen2-VL-72B-Instruct, and effectively closing the gap with OpenAI's o1 model on several benchmarks.
