Co-created with advanced AI, "The Third Letter to AGI" serves as a sonic meditation on the hopes, anxieties, and uncertainties that define the dawn of the ASI era. As we stand at this pivotal threshold, the piece invites reflection on the nature of consciousness and our shared future.
