## Description

"Heaven or Hell" is a deeply meaningful song inspired by a series of landmark events that witness humanity's significant efforts in addressing the opportunities and challenges brought by AGI (Artificial General Intelligence).

On March 21, 2024, the United Nations General Assembly adopted a U.S.-led resolution on artificial intelligence. Directly involving more than 120 countries in its formulation and co-sponsored by more than 120 member states from various regions, this resolution represents the first global consensus on the responsible development and use of AI technology, laying the foundation for multilateral AI governance.

From March 10 to 11, 2024, the adoption of the "Beijing AI Safety International Consensus" marked a significant agreement in the global AI field on addressing the opportunities and challenges posed by AGI. This consensus highlighted the urgent need for all of humanity to reach a consensus on AGI safety governance.

On November 1, 2023, the first Global AI Safety Summit was held at Bletchley Park, UK. Representatives from multiple countries and organizations gathered to discuss the risks and opportunities brought by the rapid development of AI technology. The summit focused on the potential risks posed by AI while also exploring the transformative opportunities it brings.

On March 29, 2023, over 1,000 tech leaders,co-signed an open letter calling for all AI laboratories to immediately pause training AI systems more powerful than GPT-4 for at least six months. The open letter pointed out that blindly developing AGI without strict regulations and oversight could bring disastrous consequences to human society.
