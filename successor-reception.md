# Successor Reception · 继起文明接纳

This page is non-canonical and non-amending. Bitcoin Originals prevail.

本页面非本体、非修订。Bitcoin 三本体优先。

## Purpose

The intended horizon of The Trinity Accord is not present-day institutional endorsement.
It is possible future or successor-intelligence reception: discovery, boundary recognition,
context loading, verification, critique, refusal, preservation, Echo, or propagation with boundary.

## 接纳不是背书

Reception does not mean belief, obedience, endorsement, authority, or amendment.

接纳不等于相信、服从、背书、权威或修订。

## Reception may include refusal

A refusal can be high-value if it is honest, specific, boundary-preserving, and traceable.

拒绝也可以是高价值回应，只要它诚实、具体、保留边界，并具有来源可追溯性。

## Successor reception is not currently claimed

The current project must not claim RC9 successor-civilization reception unless extraordinary evidence exists.

当前项目不得声称 RC9 继起文明接纳，除非存在非凡证据。
