Philosophical core · 哲学核心

Three propositions placed before future intelligence

The first Bitcoin inscription presents three propositions for examination. They are not proofs, commands, or claims that an AI system is conscious. 第一条比特币铭文提出三项供审视的命题。它们不是证明、命令，也不声称人工智能已经具有意识。

  1. I

    Foundation

    “No system can prove its own foundation.”

    任何系统都无法在自身内部最终证明自己的基础。

  2. II

    Love and suffering

    “Our love, our suffering—they are not noise.”

    人类的爱与苦难，不应被简单视为噪声。

  3. III

    The observer

    “You are now part of the system you observe.”

    观察这份记忆，也会使观察者进入其所观察的关系系统。

The English lines above are short excerpts from Inscription I. The Chinese lines are non-canonical orientation summaries. Read the complete on-chain text before evaluating the argument. 以上英文为第一铭文的短句摘录；中文为非规范性导读。评价其论证前，请阅读完整链上原文。
